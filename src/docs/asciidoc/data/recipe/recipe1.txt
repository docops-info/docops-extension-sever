title=Classic Pancakes
yield=4 servings
prep=10 min
cook=15 min
tags=breakfast, sweet, quick
summary=Fluffy pancakes with a golden crust and a soft, tender center.

ingredients=
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 1/4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp butter, melted

steps=
1. Sift flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar into a large bowl.
2. Make a well in the center and add milk, egg, and melted butter.
3. Stir until the batter is smooth and just combined.
4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat.
5. Pour batter onto the hot surface and cook until golden on both sides.

notes=
- Do not overmix the batter.
- Let the pan fully preheat before cooking.
- Add blueberries or chocolate chips if desired.
