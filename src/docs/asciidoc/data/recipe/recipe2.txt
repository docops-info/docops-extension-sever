title=Curry Mango
yield=4 servings
prep=15 min
cook=10 min
theme=brand
tags=vegetarian, snack, Guyanese
summary=Curry mango is a tangy, spicy snack with a balance of heat, salt, and sour fruit. Best served fresh.

ingredients=
- 2 mangoes, not fully ripe
- 1 hot pepper, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 3 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- salt to taste

steps=
1. Peel and cut the mangoes into bite-sized pieces.
2. Add the curry powder, cumin, garlic, pepper, water, and salt into a bowl and mix well.
3. Heat oil in a pan.
4. Add the spice mixture and stir until fragrant.
5. Add the mango pieces and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
6. Stir until the mango is coated and slightly softened.
7. Serve warm or at room temperature.

notes=
- Use less pepper if you want a milder version.
- Add a little more water if the mixture becomes too dry.
- This works well as a snack or a side dish.
- Serve with roti, rice, or as a standalone snack.
