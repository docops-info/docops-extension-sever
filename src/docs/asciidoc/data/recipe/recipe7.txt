title=Bartaco Sticky Pork Belly Tacos
yield=about 4 servings
prep=20 minutes
cook=45 minutes
tags=copycat, pork belly, tacos, sweet and savory
summary=
This is often considered the most popular menu item, featuring a sweet and savory glaze.

ingredients=
- 2 lbs pork belly, cubed
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon ginger paste
- Corn tortillas
- Pickled red onions
- Fresh cilantro

steps=
1. Prepare the pork belly: Cube the pork belly and toss with soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, and red pepper flakes.
2. Roast the pork: Spread the pork on a baking sheet and roast at 400°F (about 200°C) until tender and slightly crisp, reaching an internal temperature of 160°F.
3. Make the glaze: Combine 1/4 cup soy sauce, 1/4 cup hoisin sauce, 2 tablespoons brown sugar, and ginger paste in a saucepan. Simmer until sticky.
4. Assemble the tacos: Place the pork on corn tortillas, drizzle with glaze, and top with pickled red onions and cilantro.
