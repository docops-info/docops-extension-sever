A simple and satisfying pasta dish for busy evenings.

prep= 15 minutes
cook= 35 minutes
tags= soup, seasonal, healthy
summary=
A warm roasted vegetable soup with a smooth, rich texture.
ingredients=
- 2 carrots
- 1 onion
- 1 zucchini
- 2 tomatoes
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cups vegetable stock
- Salt and pepper to taste
steps=
1. Roast the vegetables until tender.
2. Transfer to a pot and add stock.
3. Simmer for 10 minutes.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve hot with crusty bread.
notes=
- Garnish with herbs or a swirl of cream.
- Add lentils for extra protein.

yield= 8 slices
prep= 20 minutes
cook= 35 minutes
tags= dessert, cake, chocolate
summary=
A rich chocolate cake with a soft crumb and simple ingredients.
ingredients=
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1 tsp baking powder
steps=
1. Preheat the oven to 180C.
2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
3. Add wet ingredients and stir until combined.
4. Pour into a greased pan and bake until set.
notes=
- Cool before serving.
- Dust with icing sugar or frost as desired.
